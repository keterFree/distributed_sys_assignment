Welcome to Our Website

Our web application is designed to provide users with a convenient platform to manage their contact details securely. Here are some of the key features:

Features:

  • User Login Page:

    • Our website has a user-friendly login page where users can securely log in using their username and password.

  • Forgot Password Functionality:

    • We provide a "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Users can reset their password by entering their email address, and a reset password message will be sent to their email.

  • Contact Detail Form:

    • Once logged in, users can view contact details, including mobile phone number, email address, physical address, and registration number.

  • Search Contacts:

    • Our website allows users to search for contacts using a person's registration number. This feature helps users quickly find and access contact details.
